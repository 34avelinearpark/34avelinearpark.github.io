Community Support for a Linear Park in Jackson Heights


Since the launch of this website in May 2021, over 2,500 people have signed our petition for a permanent park on 34th Avenue. The potential in a new open space in our Jackson Heights has the drawn the imagination and enthusiasm of our neighbors and fellow open space advocates. Some of the comments submitted to us in the petition are shared below.

We are Friends of 34th Avenue Linear Park, a collective of Jackson Heights residents from different backgrounds and different parts of the neighborhood. Among us are public school parents, students, immigrants, residents of 34th Avenue, and people who have called Jackson Heights home for their entire lives. We have banded together as volunteer organizers for a common cause:

To support the creation of a linear park on 34th Avenue.
