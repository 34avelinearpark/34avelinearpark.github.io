Our Petition

Support for a Linear Park in Jackson Heights


Since the launch of our petition in May 2021, over 2,500 people have signed on in support of a permanent park on 34th Avenue, currently home to one of the city's most successful Open Streets. As our community recovers from the Covid-19 pandemic, the potential for new, permanent greenspace in Jackson Heights has activated the imagination of our neighbors and fellow open space advocates and energized a discussion about environmental equity. A selection of the comments submitted via the the petition are shared below.

We are Friends of 34th Avenue Linear Park, a collective of Jackson Heights residents from different backgrounds and different parts of the neighborhood. Among us are public school parents, students, immigrants, residents of 34th Avenue, and people who have called Jackson Heights home for their entire lives. We have banded together as volunteer organizers for a common cause:

To support the creation of a linear park on 34th Avenue.
